I took Computer Science 1 during my Sophomore year, and we mainly focused on Swift. Throughout the year, we mainly did small assignments, but we did end up doing one major assignment at the end of the year called the ISP.

For my ISP I worked together with two of my friends to create a side scrolling platformer that took inspiration from the likes of The Dinosaur Game and Jetpack Joyride. It included music, sound effects, double jump, and fast fall.
